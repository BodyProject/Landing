OPTION 01 TRAINING AND MEALS PROGRAMME

This 4 and 6-week intensive weight loss programme combines expert one to one personal training and healthy, chef-prepared meals, and is one of a kind in Ireland.

This dual approach yields fantastic results in a short space of time and is ideal for those who need a total lifestyle overhaul, or who have significant weight loss goals.

  • Each week you’ll receive 3 personal training sessions at our Dublin 2 studio.

  • You’ll receive all of your meals for six days of each week - breakfast, lunch and dinner.

  • Each week we check your weight,body fat and key point measurements.

Lack of time, lack of motivation or simply lack of know how - it can be hard to eat healthily. We supply delicious, healthy chef-prepared meals to take the guesswork, hassle and stress out of eating well. Whether you’re a health-concsious

professional with little time to cook from scratch, or simply looking to improve your lifestyle, our meals offering is ideal. We use only the best ingredients, our meals are all naturally low GI, free from grains, gluten and refined sugars.
